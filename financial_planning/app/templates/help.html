Overview

Welcome to the Finwise Help Center! Whether you're new to our platform or a seasoned user, this page is designed to provide you with quick answers to common questions and guidance on using our services effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up for an account?
    To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the prompts to enter your information. Once completed, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
  • How can I reset my password?
    If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Change Password" which is available on the user profile page.
  • What services do you offer?
    Finwise offers a range of financial services, including budgeting tools, investment tracking, goal setting, and personalized recommendations based on your financial goals.
  • Is my financial information secure?
    We take the security of your financial information very seriously. Our website employs industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Additionally, we never store your banking credentials on our servers.

Contact Us

If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team at support@finwise.com. We're here to help!
