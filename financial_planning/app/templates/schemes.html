High-Yield Savings Accounts

High-yield savings accounts offer higher interest rates compared to traditional savings accounts.

These accounts help students grow their savings over time while keeping risk low.

Suitable for short-term savings goals.

Certificates of Deposit (CDs)

CDs offer fixed interest rates for a specific term, allowing students to save securely.

They provide predictable returns and are available in various terms.

Ideal for those who can set aside funds for a fixed period.

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs)

SIPs allow students to invest small amounts regularly in mutual funds.

This helps build a diversified portfolio over time.

Offers flexibility and low-cost investing options.

Recurring Deposits (RDs)

RDs involve making regular deposits over a specified period.

They offer a higher interest rate than traditional savings accounts.

Great for disciplined saving and future financial planning.
